How we got here

Julia and I met in 2020, and with no restaurants, bars, or movie theaters open, we spent a lot of time talking. Talking, it seems, is one of the tricks to falling in love, and by the end of the year we were head over heels. 2021 saw us grow closer. Getting to know each other's friends and families, backpacking in Yosemite, and dressing as cockroaches for Halloween. At the start of 2022 we moved in together. There, Linus and Scamp met Kosh, and Julia was forced to reckon with the reality that she now lived in a one bedroom apartment with three cats. By 2023, we knew we were ready to spend the rest of our lives together, so we found some rings and headed to City Clerk's office. With paperwork in hand, we picked an evening, gathered some friends, said some words, licked the envelope and sent it off. Two weeks later we found ourselves to be an officially married couple, and it's been bliss since.
